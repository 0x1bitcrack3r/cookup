Frequently Asked Questions

Why the name CookUp?
We deliver ingredients in available amount required for cooking as per servings. CookUp goes along with cooking and strongly reflects what we do. So is the name “CookUp.com”
What comes with each recipe box ?
We deliver all the raw ingredients ( apart from oil and salt ) in exact amounts (as per servings) required for cooking and along with it a simple cooking instruction (manual).
What are the measures you take to keep ingredients fresh ?
All the perishables with low shelf lives like those of vegetables, paneer are bought on the same day of delivery and are stored in our refrigeration systems until the time of delivery. Chicken is sourced from the local and trusted vendor, thus its' always super-fresh. We only deliver fresh ingredients.
What is the shelf life of the ingredients you provide ?
It varies from one ingredient to another as all of them have different shelf lives. We will be providing you with a sheet along with the recipes box that will have information about the shelf life of that box. In case of any doubt you can always enquire with the delivery boy on this issue or give us a call.
How can I be sure about the quality of ingredients you deliver ?
“Quality is not an act its a habit” -- we strongly believe in this, and we never compromise on quality. The ingredients delivered are hand-picked & chosen carefully to assure superior quality. We also have a cash back guarantee, in case you find any of the ingredients is of inferior quality, report it to us and you will get your entire money back.
What equipment and ingredients do you assume I have at home?
For ingredients, all you need is salt and vegatable oil. As far as cooking equipments are concerned, we assume you will have standard pots, pans and knives. For recipes that require presence of special equipments (like oven, microwave or mixer), additional information in form of notes are displayed with the recipe on the menu page. We’ll provide you with everything else!
How long will it take to deliver?
We have fixed delivery slots. The slot timings will appear on the checkout page. Choose a suitable slot based on your availability and need. Your recipe box will reach your doorstep between the selected slot timings.
What are the delivery charges ?
Delivery is free and will always be.
Do you also do same day delivery?
No. You have to order a day in advance.
